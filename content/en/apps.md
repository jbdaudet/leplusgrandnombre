## Our solutions for craftsmen

### Artisans Manager

> The all-in-one tool to manage your craft business.

**[artisans.leplusgrandnombre.fr](https://artisans.leplusgrandnombre.fr)**

A comprehensive application for building and industrial craftsmen, covering 18 trades:

- **Project management** — Create and track your projects with AI assistance
- **Manufacturing processes** — Automatically generate your fabrication steps
- **Professional quotes** — Create and export your quotes as PDF
- **Inventory management** — Track your tools, consumables and materials
- **Scheduling** — Integrated calendar with automatic planning
- **Client directory** — Manage your contacts and client history

**Supported trades:** Carpenter, Cabinetmaker, Timber Framer, Plumber, Electrician, Mason, Painter, Tiler, Plasterer, Roofer, Locksmith, Metalworker, Welder, Mechanic, Auto Body Worker, Glazier, Upholsterer, Marble Worker.
