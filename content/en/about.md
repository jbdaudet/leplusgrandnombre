## Our mission

**Le Plus Grand Nombre** is a company dedicated to the digital transformation of craftsmen and building professionals in France. We believe that digital tools should not be reserved for large companies — every craftsman deserves modern, simple and affordable solutions.

## What we do

We develop **custom web applications** that address the real needs of craftsmen:

- **Project management** — Track your projects from start to finish
- **Quotes and invoicing** — Create professional quotes in just a few clicks
- **Inventory management** — Keep control of your tools and consumables
- **Scheduling** — Organize your calendar and your team's planning
- **Artificial intelligence** — Let AI help you design your manufacturing processes

## Our approach

We work closely with craftsmen to understand their real needs. Every feature is designed to save time and simplify daily work.

Our applications are:
- **Hosted in France** (AWS Paris) for performance and compliance
- **Secured** with industry best practices
- **Scalable** to adapt to your growing business
